May – June 2026 Training Course Spain · UPV/EHU

AI for Water Resources: Training Course in Spain

The Ai-LEARN consortium is running its inaugural intensive training course on AI-powered water resources modelling, hosted by UPV/EHU in the Basque Country. Participants work hands-on with LSTM flood-forecasting models, satellite hydrology data pipelines, and the AURA-Learn AI tutoring framework — guided by consortium researchers from all three partner institutions.

May 2026 Launch

Project Website Goes Live

The official Ai-LEARN project website launches, providing open access to project information, AURA-Learn documentation, partner resources, and news for the broader water science and AI education communities.

🚀
October 2025 Milestone

Ai-LEARN Project Officially Launched

The consortium held its inaugural kick-off meeting, bringing together researchers from IHE Delft, UPV/EHU, and TUC to align on the project roadmap, governance structure, and first-year activities.

📋
November 2025 Agreement

Partnership Agreement Signed by All Three Institutions

The formal Consortium Agreement was signed by IHE Delft, UPV/EHU, and TUC, establishing the governance framework, intellectual property management, and financial arrangements for the 36-month collaboration.

🔬
February 2026 Deliverable

Case Study Technical Toolkits Completed

All three regional case study technical toolkits were delivered on schedule: integrated data pipelines for the Greek islands groundwater study, Iberian satellite hydrology, and Latin American watershed flood forecasting.
