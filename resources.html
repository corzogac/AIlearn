Resources & Deliverables

Key outputs, milestones, and open educational resources across the 36-month project lifecycle. This page is updated as outputs become available.

⚙️

Project underway — results being generated

Ai-LEARN started in October 2025. Key results from the first reporting period will be published here as they become available. Expected first outputs include the items listed below.

○
Ai-LEARN AI Toolbox Suite of TensorFlow/Keras models for flood forecasting, groundwater infilling, and drought assessment
○
Educational LLM Integration Framework Prompting protocols, instructor guides, and student interaction templates for AI-assisted learning
○
AURA-Learn Teaching Framework Three-agent pedagogical system: student guide, teacher assistant, and independent evaluator
○
WaterTech Learning Portal Internationally accessible virtual learning environment with multilingual course materials
○
3 Case Study Datasets & Analysis Greek islands groundwater, Iberian hydrology, and Latin American watershed flood forecasting
○
6+ Co-authored Publications High-impact journal articles on AI for environmental education and water science
○
Open Educational Resources All learning materials freely available under open licences for the global academic community
